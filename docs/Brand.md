# What is AklaCholo?

> **AklaCholo** — _Bengal, Everywhere._
>
> A global Bengali creative universe that transforms the soul of Bengal — its
> kolka, craft, textiles, food, and storytelling — into a modern lifestyle
> experience powered by creativity, community, and technology.

This is the brand's north star: the vision, values, and the story behind the
work. It also draws a clear line between **what AklaCholo is** as a brand and
**what the app is today** (Phase 1), so anyone building it knows the scope.

_Source: synthesized from the founder's vision & executive-summary documents._

---

## Vision

AklaCholo is a global Bengali creative universe where **tradition meets
innovation, and Bengal becomes a global design language.** It blends culture,
craft, and technology into a modern lifestyle brand — bringing Bengal's artistic
soul (kolka, textiles, folklore, home décor, and food) to a worldwide audience
through immersive design, ethical production, and AI-powered creativity.

The ambition: a world where Bengal is **not a hidden gem but a global force** in
design, culture, and creativity — where kolka motifs live in homes from Boston
to Berlin, artisans thrive, animators innovate, and culture becomes commerce.

## Mission

To **elevate Bengal's artistic heritage** by empowering artisans, AI animators,
designers, and cultural creators with a global platform. AklaCholo brings
together culture, commerce, and creativity to build a **sustainable, ethical,
and future-facing ecosystem** — making Bengal's creativity accessible,
desirable, and economically empowering on a global scale.

## The story

AklaCholo was born from a simple truth: **Bengal's creativity is world-class,
yet globally invisible.** Its kolka motifs, textiles, folklore, and soul were
scattered across continents — unorganized, unseen, and undervalued.

The founder — shaped by Bengali roots and global leadership in healthcare and AI
— carried Bengal within her yet saw how absent it remained from the world's
cultural imagination. AklaCholo emerged from that tension: a desire to build a
**home for Bengal's creativity** — not as nostalgia, but as a modern, global,
future-facing identity. It bridges the gap by uniting artisans, AI animators,
and cultural storytellers into one creative ecosystem, and turns kolka into a
global design language.

## What AklaCholo is — and what it isn't

**AklaCholo IS:**
- A **creative universe** — a living world where home décor, kolka art, textiles,
  Bengali food, animation, classes, and creator gigs coexist.
- **Discovery-first** — users explore, learn, and fall in love with Bengal one
  interaction at a time, before they ever buy.
- A **cross-border creator economy** that gives artisans and designers
  visibility, dignity, fair compensation, and global reach — turning creators
  into cultural ambassadors.
- **Modern and future-facing** — heritage craft expressed through contemporary,
  minimal, premium design.
- **App-first** — the app is the cultural home of the brand, not just a store.

**AklaCholo IS NOT:**
- **Not just a marketplace / e-commerce store.** Commerce is one pillar inside a
  cultural universe — not the whole point.
- **Not nostalgia or a museum.** Bengal is presented as a modern global identity,
  not a relic to preserve under glass.
- **Not a reseller of generic "ethnic" goods.** Every piece carries its maker,
  its craft, and its story.
- **Not cluttered or transactional.** The experience is calm, immersive, and
  visual — heritage as a lifestyle, not a catalog.

## The creator ecosystem

At the heart of AklaCholo are its creators: **artisans, AI animators, designers,
and teachers** united into a cross-border creative economy. They earn through
product sales, classes, gigs, and collaborations. The platform provides
visibility, fair compensation, and global reach — transforming creators into
cultural ambassadors.

**Gig + class model:** creators take on gigs (kolka animation, product design,
home-décor styling, teaching) and run classes (kolka art, embroidery, AI
animation, Bengali cooking, storytelling) — building community and recurring
income.

## App-first strategy

The AklaCholo app is the **cultural home** of the brand and the gateway into the
universe: discovery-first shopping, animated kolka experiences, creator
portfolios, classes, and gig opportunities. It is where users explore, learn,
and shop — and where creators teach, animate, design, and earn.

## Product roadmap (the full vision)

| Phase | Focus |
|---|---|
| **Phase 1 — Discovery App** | A discovery-first mobile app: Bengal-inspired exploration, kolka motif selectors, curated collections, and creator profiles. |
| **Phase 2 — Marketplace + Gigs + Classes** | A creator marketplace offering products, services, and classes; gig opportunities for artisans, animators, and designers. |
| **Phase 3 — Home Décor + Bengal in a Box** | Physical product lines (embroidered pillows, kolka trays, terracotta kitchenware) and themed cultural subscription boxes. |
| **Phase 4 — AI Stylist + AR + Premium Lines** | AI-powered home styling, AR visualization, and premium collections (hand-painted sarees, limited-edition textiles). |

## Business model

A **hybrid model** spanning digital and physical offerings, with diverse revenue
streams:
- Physical products (home décor, fashion)
- Digital products (animations, design assets)
- Subscriptions (Bengal in a Box)
- Classes (art, cooking, animation)
- Creator commissions / gig marketplace fees
- Premium app features, brand collaborations, and cultural experiences/events

## Why now — market opportunity

Global consumers are seeking **cultural authenticity, ethical production, and
personalized experiences.** The creator economy is booming and AI is
transforming creative work. Bengal's rich artistic heritage is uniquely
positioned to meet this demand. AklaCholo sits at the intersection of **culture,
creativity, and technology** — ready to define a new category of global
lifestyle brand.

**Competitive advantage:** kolka as a global design language; a network of
skilled artisans and AI animators; a discovery-first experience; ethical
production; and a cross-border creator economy.

## What the app is *today* (Phase 1 reality)

This repository implements **Phase 1: the Discovery App** — the first chapter of
the vision above. It deliberately ships discovery and storytelling first, and
leaves commerce and the creator economy for later phases.

**Built now:** a visual, calm, premium discovery experience — Home feed,
**Discover** (browse Bengal-inspired design by category, with "The Magic of
Bengal" stories woven in), **Creators** (artisan/designer/teacher profiles),
**Saved** (wishlist), collection/article/product detail screens, and an
interactive **Kolka Studio** (motif + palette explorer).

**Intentionally NOT built yet (later phases):** Shop/commerce, marketplace/gigs,
classes, Bengal-in-a-Box, AI stylist/AR, and the Phase 1.5 grounded chat.
Pricing shown in the app is display-only — there is no checkout.

> Note: the brand vision describes "room-based exploration"; Phase 1 realizes
> this as **category-based discovery** (Kitchen, Living, Stationery, Wearables,
> Wall Art) with plain-English navigation labels for a global audience. The
> Bengali soul lives in the brand name and the content, not in the menu labels.
